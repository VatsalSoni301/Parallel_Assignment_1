Here m*n and n*p are dimensions of the matrices

1. Dense matrix

	Flops = 2*m*n*p
	Space complexity = O(m*n)

2. Banded matrix

	Flops = 2*(p1+q1+1)*(p2+q2+1)*p
	Space complexity = O(m*n)

3. Coordinate Storage Format

	Flops = 2*m*n*p
	Space complexity = O(m*n)

4. Compressed Sparse Row

	Flops = 2*m*n*p
	Space complexity = O(m*n)
